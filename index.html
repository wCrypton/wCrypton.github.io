Welcome to Slend Sorcery

Slend Sorcery is a world where magic is cards and cards are magic. Play as a young adventurer, eager to explore ancient dungeons and find long-forgotten cards to build the legendary spell deck that could defeat the one wielding the forbidden cards the entire world fears!




Features

- A rank system to have rewards and be in the top of the world!
- Daily missions with rewards every day!
- Several characters with different levels, different items and different skills!
- Receive messages and gifts in real time from your friends!
+8 world and level available for beta, test the game now! And more in the future!
